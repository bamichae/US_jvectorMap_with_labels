Clickable US vector map with labels

  • Many thanks to jvectorMap
  • As seen here and here
  • Click map to select options below

SVG was created in Inkscape. All labels and leader lines were converted to paths and 'sandwiched' between 2 identical layers of polygons representing the State and territory boundaries. The bottom poly layer is styled/colored with CSS and the top poly layer is transparent and clickable.The jvectorMap used (USMAP.js) was created using this svg to jvectorMap tool. Bad things happen in IE8 and below. . .
